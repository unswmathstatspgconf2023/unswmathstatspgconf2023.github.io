Research Translation in All Its Forms

Join us for the 10th annual UNSW Mathematics and Statistics Postgraduate Conference! See our students showcase the diverse and cutting-edge research they are achieving right now.

About the Conference

This year's Postgraduate Conference will feature a range of student presentations over the day, showcasing the diverse and innovative research flowing from our research master and PhD student cohorts. The schedule includes longer plenary sessions and talks by students that have recently been presented at overseas conferences. The day provides an opportunity for our postgraduate research students to present the research they are currently working on. We will also host an after-party for all attendees in the Colombo Building's foyer.

  • Date: Friday 11 August
  • Time: 8.50am-4.30pm
  • Venue: Colombo Theatres, UNSW Sydney (map reference: B16)
