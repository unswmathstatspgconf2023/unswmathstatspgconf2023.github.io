Research Translation in All Its Forms

The theme of the conference is Research Translation in All Its Forms. Research translation encompasses the systematic and interdisciplinary process of narrowing the divide between scientific knowledge and its practical application across multiple domains. It entails the efficient conversion of research findings into concrete outcomes that can yield societal benefits. This process encompasses diverse modalities, including knowledge dissemination, technology transfer, implementation science, and dissemination strategies. From a scientific standpoint, research translation seeks to optimise the transfer of knowledge by employing rigorous methodologies and evidence-based practices, while considering contextual factors and engaging relevant stakeholders. By actively fostering the integration of research findings into policies, programs, and interventions, research translation facilitates the expeditious advancement of scientific knowledge, fosters innovation, and maximises the societal impact of scientific discoveries.

About the Conference

This year's Postgraduate Conference will spotlight our master's and PhD students' innovative and diverse research through a variety of presentations. The schedule includes plenary sessions and talks by students recently featured at international conferences. The event, culminating in an after-party at the Colombo Building's foyer, provides a stage for our students to share their current research.
